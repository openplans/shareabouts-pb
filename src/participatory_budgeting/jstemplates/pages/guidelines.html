{{#_}}Guidelines{{/_}}

  • {{#_}}Anyone can post ideas, even if you don’t live in this Council District.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Eligible ideas must be for "capital" projects: physical infrastructure for public benefit, such as park improvements or new technology for schools. “Expense" projects, such as afterschool programs or expanding bus service, are not eligible.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}To add a spot, drag the map until the crosshairs are over the desired location. Ideas should be located within the Council District boundaries.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}All ideas must be submitted by November 11th, 2013.{{/_}}
